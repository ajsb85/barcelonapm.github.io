mod_perl 2.0 By Example

We, the people from Barcelona Perl Mongers with Oasyssoft support, have just setup the III Barcelona Perl Festival on July 14th, related to mod_perl, the Perl extension of Apache web server. Stas Bekman, co-author of this program, will give a seminar about the popular module, three hours long. Free entrance.

You're welcome to join us!
