NAME

Barcelona.pm - Barcelona Perl Mongers

SYNOPSIS

use Barcelona;

DESCRIPTION

Barcelona.pm is a group of people interested in Perl (a.k.a. Perl Mongers) that are living in Barcelona. Interestingly enough, there's a lot of people in the group that happen to be living outside Barcelona too, so anyone is welcome aboard. Please, start subscribing the mailing list and attending the meetings the last Thursday of each month at Viena Pelai.

CAVEATS

Perl can become very, very addictive! Don't mix it with other drugs and ask your doctor in case of intoxication.
